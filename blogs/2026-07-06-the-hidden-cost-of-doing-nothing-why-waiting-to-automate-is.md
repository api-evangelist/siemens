What’s holding crane automation back? Studies show that nearly 40% of crane-related accidents on the shopfloor happen due to load sway, a hazard that can be avoided by using automated anti-sway controls. The integration of crane systems and their digital warehouse management systems enables real-time tracking, optimized stacking, and predictive scheduling.
